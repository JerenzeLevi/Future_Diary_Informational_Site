KAMADO UESHITA

Kamado Ueshita (上下 かまど, Ueshita Kamado) is a large, strangely shaped woman in charge of an orphanage named "Mother's Village". Kamado's diary seems to be in the form of a website where she can rent out blog space to others and can automatically promote its users' rank based on the number of posts the user makes.
